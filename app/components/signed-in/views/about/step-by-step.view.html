How it works, step by step

Not sure how exactly Dealers works from start to finish? Here's a full guide. If you have any other question, don't hesitate - ask us!

1

Sign up and provide your or your business info.

This includes details like a photo of you or your business, your name, and your bank account info.

2

Your in! Time to add some products

Once your signed in as a dealer you'll see a plus icon in the navigation bar. That's how you upload a new product.

3

Spread the word

Once you uploaded a new product, you can share it in your Facebook account, whether it's your private profile or business page. When your customers click the link, they will be directed straight to the product's page in Dealers, one step before purchase.

4

Receive orders

Now that you have beautiful links directing customers from your Facebook page to your Dealers product page, you can start receiving orders online. Whenever a customer purchases one of your products, you'll be notified by email.

5

Track your sales in your profile

All the sales you made will be listed in your Dealers profile, under the sales tab. There you will see all the information regarding each one of the purchases, including quantity, shipping address, and preferred shipping method.

6

Send your products to your customers

All that left to be done is send your products. Go over each sale and send the right package according to the customer's preferences. Regarding the shipment company, these are our recommendations:

  • Zig-Zag
  • Ratz Catzvi ("רץ כצבי")
  • Israel Postal Company (דואר ישראל)

After you send it, you can update the status of the purchase to notify the customer that the product is on its way.

7

Receiving your sales income

At the end of each month, you receive the total income of all the done sales you made. A sale is considered done when one of the following occurs:

  1. The customer changed the status of the purchase to Received
  2. Three weeks have passed since the purchase took place

That's it! We're always happy to help with further questions you might have. Send us an email for more information.
