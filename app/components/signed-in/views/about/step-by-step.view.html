How it works, step by step

Not sure exactly how Dealers works? Here's a full guide from start to finish. If you have any other questions, please do not hesitate - ask us!

1

Sign up and provide your or your business' info.

This includes details such as photo of you or your business, your name, and your bank account info.

2

You're in! Time to add some products

Once you are signed up as a dealer you'll see a plus icon in the navigation bar. By clicking the icon you can upload a new product: add a photo of the product, tell us about it, choose a price, etc.

3

Spread the word

Once you uploaded a new product, you can share it on Facebook with a rich media link, whether it's your private profile or business page. By clicking the link, your customers will be directed to the Dealers product page, where they are one step away from purchasing the product.

4

Receive orders

Now that you have rich media links directing your customers from Facebook to your Dealers product page, you can begin receiving orders online. You'll be notified by email with each new purchase.

5

Track your sales in your profile

All the sales you made will be listed in your Dealers profile, under the sales tab. There you will see all the information regarding each purchase, including quantity, shipping address, and preferred shipping method.

6

Send your products to your customers

All that is left to be done is to send your products. Go over each sale and send the right package according to the customer's requests. We recommend the following shipping companies:

  • Zig-Zag
  • Ratz Catzvi ("רץ כצבי")
  • Israel Postal Company (דואר ישראל)

After the product is sent, you can update the status of the purchase to notify the customer that the product is on its way.

7

Receiving your payment

At the end of each month, you receive the total income of all the completed sales you made. A sale is considered completed when one of the following occurs:

  1. The customer changed the status of the purchase to Received
  2. Three weeks have passed since the purchase took place

That's it! We're always happy to help with any further questions you might have. Send us an email for more information.
